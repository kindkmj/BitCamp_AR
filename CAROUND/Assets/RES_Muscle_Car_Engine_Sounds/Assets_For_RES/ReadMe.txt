If you want to use Muscle Car Engine Sounds with Realistic Engine Sounds asset,
firstly import Realistic Engine Sounds and later RES_MuscleCar.unitypackage.
This unitypackage contain a demo scene and prefabs for Realistic Engine Sounds.
